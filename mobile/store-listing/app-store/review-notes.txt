### How to test core features
1. Sign in with the demo account.
2. Open **书库** (Home) → tap any book → **开始阅读**.
3. In the reader, toggle **结伴/独自** in settings (gear icon).
4. Select text → add a margin note; notes from other users appear beside paragraphs.
5. Scroll to chapter end → post a chapter review.
6. **发现** tab → browse recommendations and reading groups.
7. **举报** is available on book detail and in the reader for UGC compliance.
