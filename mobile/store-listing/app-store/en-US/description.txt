Peidu is an atmospheric social reading community. Unlike speed-reading apps, we focus on quiet immersion and the feeling that someone else is on the same page—literally.

【Core experience】
• Together / Solo toggle — switch companionship on or off with one tap
• Anchored margin notes — thoughts appear beside the exact sentence, not in a generic comment thread
• Live presence — see how many readers are in this chapter right now
• Ambient reading — dusk, paper, and night themes; rain, fireplace, and café soundscapes
• Chapter reviews — share how a chapter felt; brighten others' insights

【Library & creation】
• Public-domain classics with search and resume reading
• Author dashboard for original works
• Bookshelf and cloud-synced progress

【Light social】
• Follow readers you enjoy
• Reading groups that move at the same pace
• Discover recommendations and group calendars

【Trust & privacy】
• Report button and safe-harbor compliance
• Private notes visible only to you
• Full privacy policy in-app

Peidu believes reading was never meant to be lonely. Welcome aboard.
