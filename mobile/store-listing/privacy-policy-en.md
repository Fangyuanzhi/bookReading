# Peidu Privacy Policy

**Effective date: June 26, 2026**  
**Last updated: June 26, 2026**

Peidu ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

By using Peidu you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the service.

---

## 1. Information we collect

### 1.1 Information you provide
- **Account**: email, display name, optional avatar
- **User content**: margin notes, chapter reviews, private notes, original chapters, bookshelf and reading progress
- **Reports**: content you flag and related descriptions

### 1.2 Information collected automatically
- **Device & logs**: device model, OS version, app version, crash reports
- **Usage data**: reading progress and feature usage (for resume reading and core features)
- **Network**: IP address for security and abuse prevention (not used for precise location)

### 1.3 We do not collect
- Precise geolocation
- Contacts, SMS, or call logs
- Biometric data
- Photo library / camera without your action (except avatar upload you initiate)

---

## 2. How we use information

| Purpose | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Core service | Reading, margin notes, presence, bookshelf, social features |
| Security | Authentication, fraud prevention |
| Improvement | Aggregated analytics, crash fixes |
| Compliance | Safe-harbor reporting workflow |
| Notifications (optional) | New notes, follow activity (can be disabled) |

---

## 3. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:
- **Service providers** (hosting, storage, realtime messaging) under data-processing agreements
- **Legal requests** when required by law
- **Safety** to prevent fraud, abuse, or harm

**Private notes** are never shown to other users or third parties.

---

## 4. Storage & security

Data is stored on servers in your deployment region. We use HTTPS, access controls, and backups. After account deletion, we remove or anonymize personal data within a reasonable period unless law requires retention.

---

## 5. Your rights

You may access, correct, or delete your content in-app; request account deletion via support@peidu.app; opt out of optional notifications; and request a copy of your basic account data.

---

## 6. Children

Peidu is intended for users aged 16+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

---

## 7. Local storage

We use local storage for auth tokens and reading preferences. Clear app data or sign out to remove local cache.

---

## 8. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be announced in-app or on our website.
